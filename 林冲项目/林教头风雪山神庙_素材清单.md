## 角色类素材 (Characters)

### C01 — 林冲·正面全身立绘

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, a heroic Chinese warrior standing in a solemn pose, full body front view. Lin Chong (Panther Head) from Water Margin, former instructor of 800,000 imperial guards. He has a stern square face, thick eyebrows, intense determined eyes with hidden anguish, a well-groomed black beard. Age around 35. He wears traditional Song dynasty armor - dark iron lamellar vest over blue-gray Hanfu robes, a black fur-lined cloak draped over shoulders, black leather combat boots. A long spear held in right hand. His expression is proud but weary, bearing the weight of injustice. Ink splatter effects on edges. Muted color palette with blue-gray-black tones. Clean background. Full body character design sheet style, highly detailed costume design.

### C02 — 林冲·披蓑衣雪中背影

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, Lin Chong from Water Margin, back view, full body. He wears a traditional woven straw raincoat (suoyi) over his shoulders, broad bamboo hat on head. Standing alone in heavy snowfall, snowflakes visible in the air. His back is turned to camera, posture lonely and stoic. The spear is strapped to his back. Environment is minimal snowy landscape. Ink wash texture with white snow on gray background. Melancholic atmosphere.

### C03 — 林冲·持枪怒目特写

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, close-up portrait of Lin Chong. His eyes are wide with burning rage, eyebrows furrowed fiercely. Teeth clenched in anger. Veins visible on forehead. Hand gripping spear tightly in foreground. Face illuminated by warm firelight from below. Dramatic lighting with deep shadows. Ink splatter explosion effects around head. Color palette shifts to fiery reds and blacks to symbolize rage. Intense emotional expression.

### C04 — 林冲·山神庙内饮酒

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-sheding, medium shot of Lin Chong sitting cross-legged on stone floor. He's holding a wine gourd (hulu) in one hand, bringing it to his lips. Face illuminated by warm orange firelight from a small fire pit nearby. His expression is weary but alert. He wears his armor without the cloak. Background shows ancient stone temple walls with faded paintings. Atmospheric smoke from fire. Cozy but tense interior mood.

### C05 — 陆谦·阴狡特写

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, portrait of Lu Qian (Lin Chong's traitorous former friend). He has a thin sly face, narrow cunning eyes, a slight sneer of a smile. He wears rich Song dynasty official robes in dark purple and gold. Age around 35. Expression is deceitful and calculating. Soft indirect lighting. Ink wash background with subtle snake-like patterns. His appearance contrasts with Lin Chong's honesty - he looks untrustworthy.

### C06 — 陆谦·全身立绘

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, Lu Qian from Water Margin, full body standing pose. He wears elaborate dark purple and gold official Song dynasty robes, a black official hat (futou). His posture is slightly hunched, hands clasped together in a scheming gesture. A dagger is partially visible tucked in his belt. He stands in snowy outdoors background. His face has a sinister smile. Muted cold color palette.

### C07 — 打手·雪中蒙面人

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, anonymous assassin/henchman, full body. He wears common dark gray Song dynasty clothing, face covered with black cloth mask. Holds a burning torch in one hand, sword in other. Standing in snow at night. Dangerous and threatening presence. Minimal features to emphasize anonymous minion status. Ink wash texture with snow effects.

## 场景类素材 (Scenes)

### S01 — 沧州草料场·大雪全景

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, panoramic landscape of a hay and fodder storage depot in ancient China. Large stacks of hay and straw arranged in rows, covered in thick white snow. Wooden fences and simple structures in background. Heavy snowfall filling the sky. Solitary and desolate atmosphere. Misty mountains visible in far distance. Gray and white color palette with touches of brown from the hay. Wide vertical composition for 9:16 format.

### S02 — 草料场·被雪压塌后

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, the same hay depot after collapse. Broken wooden structures and crushed hay piles scattered on the ground. Snow covering the debris. Smoke or steam rising faintly from some hay. Signs of structural failure - broken beams, split fences. Darker mood than S01. Gray and desolate. Forensic disaster scene atmosphere.

### S03 — 山神庙·外观

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, exterior of an old mountain temple (Shenmiao). Small ancient Chinese temple structure with curved roof covered in snow. Stone steps leading up to temple entrance. Old wooden doors with faded red paint. Temple sits on mountainside with pine trees covered in snow. Night time with faint moonlight. Mysterious and slightly eerie atmosphere. Dark blue and gray color palette.

### S04 — 山神庙·内景

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, interior of ancient mountain temple. Stone walls with faded murals of deities. Stone floor. A small fire pit in center with warm orange flames casting shadows. An old weathered statue of a mountain god in background. Broken wooden door visible through entrance showing snow outside. Dramatic lighting contrast between warm fire and cold night. Atmospheric smoke.

### S05 — 风雪山路·夜景

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, a mountain path at night in heavy snow. Ancient Chinese path winding through mountains. Pine trees bent under snow weight. Moon barely visible through thick clouds and snow. Footprints in snow. Dramatic lighting with cool blues and whites. Treacherous and dangerous atmosphere. Vertical composition for 9:16.

### S06 — 雪地打斗场景

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, snowy battleground after confrontation. Footprints and scuff marks in snow disrupting the pristine surface. A fallen torch on ground sputtering. A spear stuck in snow. Red blood stains on white snow (artistic ink splash style). Dramatic chaos frozen in time. The aftermath of violent conflict. Stark contrast between white snow, black ink, and red blood.

## 道具类素材 (Props)

### P01 — 林冲长枪

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, a traditional Chinese spear (qiang). Long wooden shaft with dark finish, metal spearhead with intricate designs, red tassel (ma) attached just below the blade. The spear is standing vertically or at an angle. Isolated on clean background or subtle snowy background. Weapon looks battle-worn but well-maintained. Heroic weapon aura.

### P02 — 酒葫芦

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, a traditional Chinese wine gourd (hulu) made from dried calabash. Light brown color with a fabric cord tied around the neck for carrying. It has an aged, weathered texture. Small and intimate object. Isolated or held by character. Symbol of momentary escape from harsh reality.

### P03 — 火把

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, a burning torch. Wooden handle with wrapped cloth on top engulfed in bright orange flames. Smoke and sparks rising. The fire illuminates surrounding darkness. Warm glow effect. Night use context. Tool of destruction (for burning hay depot).

### P04 — 蓑衣

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, traditional Chinese straw raincoat (suoyi). Woven from straw and palm fibers, tan and brown colors. Texture of woven fibers clearly visible. Folded or draped form. Protective gear against rain and snow. Symbol of hermit/exile life.

### P05 — 古庙破旧木门

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-sheding, old wooden temple door. Weathered gray wood with cracks and peeling faded red paint. Iron door handles and hinges. The door is slightly ajar, showing darkness beyond. Ancient and abandoned feel. Architectural detail.

## 使用说明

1. **复制每个素材的完整英文prompt到图像生成工具**（如 Nana Banana Pro）
2. **生成尺寸**：9:16 竖屏（与视频比例一致）
3. **风格一致性**：所有素材都使用相同的水墨武侠风格前缀
4. **去除水印**：生成后记得使用 banana.ovo.re 去除水印
5. **编号管理**：生成后按编号重命名文件，方便后续引用
